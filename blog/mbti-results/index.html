MBTI Results

Welp, I took another one of those quiz. My result is INFP.

In short, this label is generally:

  • Introverted
  • Likes to See the Big Picture
  • Dominant Trait favors Feeling over Thinking
  • ...and Prefers to Live in the Moment

I dunno about "Type being Permanent" because mistype is a thing, so as the weighing scale shifting from one side to another (i.e., life experience). I also dunno if the MBTI thing is a serious thing or just for fun.

The timeline of my mistypes to current type follows:
ISTP > ISFP > *Years Gap* > INTP > INFP

In Conclusion, I wanted to think I'm a INxP, but INFP suits me better. I know because I imagine viewing myself in an otherwordly perspective. It's shocking that the more accurate I answer the questions, the better the results. Although, INTP sounds superior to INFP, but that's just stereotype. I can think smart too, but leans to feeling since it's easier to relate and better for me.
